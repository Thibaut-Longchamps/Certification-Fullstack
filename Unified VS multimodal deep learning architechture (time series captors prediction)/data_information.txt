# MovePort Dataset – Data Information

This is the open access **MovePort dataset** published with our paper:  
*"MovePort: Multimodal Dataset of EMG, IMU, MoCap and Insole Pressure for Analyzing Abnormal Movements and Postures in Rehabilitation Training"*  

**Please cite this article when using the dataset.**

---

## Part 1 – File Structure

Data is stored in the `data/` folder with the following structure:

data/
├─ 1
│ └─ ...
├─ 2
│ └─ ...
...
└─ 25
├─ back
├─ forward
├─ halfsquat
├─ still
├─ ground_gait # Only for the subject with spastic paraplegia
├─ treadmill_normal
├─ treadmill_leghigh
└─ treadmill_dragging


- Each folder `1-25` represents a participant.

---

## Part 2 – Experimental Paradigm

### Experiment 1 – Treadmill Gaits
- **Normal Gait (`treadmill_normal`)**: 3 speeds  
  - Low Speed: 1 km/h  
  - Medium Speed: 2 km/h  
  - High Speed: 3–4 km/h (participant chosen)  
- **Dragging Gait (`treadmill_dragging`)**: Simulates difficulty lifting legs, 1 km/h  
- **High Leg Lift Gait (`treadmill_leghigh`)**: Simulates exaggerated leg lifts, 2 km/h  

### Experiment 2 – Ground Postures
- **Standing still (`still`)** – standard standing  
- **Forward Lean (`forward`)** – forward-weighted posture  
- **Backward Lean (`back`)** – backward-weighted posture  
- **Half Squat (`halfsquat`)** – half-squat position

## Part 3 – Data Files

- Naming format: `modality_SegmentIndex.csv` / `.avi`  
- Modalities: EMG, IMU, IPS, COP, MoCap  

### 1. Electromyography (EMG)
- Modality: `emg`  
- Data Size: 16 × nframe  
- Sampling Rate: 2000 Hz  
- Unit: Microvolts  
- Muscles (L/R):  
  - Vlat – Vastus Lateralis  
  - RF – Rectus Femoris  
  - ST – Semitendinosus  
  - TA – Tibialis Anterior  
  - MG – Medial Gastrocnemius  
  - LG – Lateral Gastrocnemius  
  - SOL – Soleus  
  - IL – Iliopsoas  

### 2. Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU)
- Modality: `imu`  
- Data Size: 54 × nframe  
- Sampling Rate: 100 Hz  
- Units: Acceleration (m/s²), Angular Velocity (°/s), Euler Angles (°)  
- Positions: Head, Waist, L/R Hand, L/R Foot  
- Axes: Acc_X/Y/Z, Gyr_X/Y/Z, Roll, Pitch, Yaw  

### 3. Insole Pressure Sensors (IPS)
- Modality: `ips`  
- Data Size: 682 × nframe  
- Sampling Rate: 60 Hz  
- Unit: psi  
- Notes: 2 electrode arrays per foot (31×11), 230 active electrodes, others = 0  

#### Center of Pressure (COP)
- Data Size: 6 × nframe  
- Units: N, m  
- Columns: L/R_Force, L/R_cop_x, L/R_cop_y  
- Note: Coordinate system consistent with MoCap

### 4. Motion Capture (MoCap)
- Modality: `mocap`  
- Data Size: 78 × nframe  
- Sampling Rate: 100 Hz  
- Unit: meters  
- 26 markers (x/y/z coordinates):
